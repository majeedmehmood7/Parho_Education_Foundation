What We Offer

To create a future where every child, regardless of their background, has access to innovative, technology-driven education, empowering them to become leaders, innovators, and changemakers in their communities and beyond.

Project-Based Learning

Emphasizing hands-on, experiential learning to engage students and develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Technology Integration

Introducing robotics, AI, and other advanced technologies to prepare students for the jobs of the future.

Leadership Development

Training students to become future leaders while empowering teachers to become mentors and role models.

Technical Institute for Children

We aim to establish a technical institute offering a range of short courses tailored to the interests and future career paths of students. This program will combine online and physical classes to maximize accessibility and learning outcomes.

Teacher Training

Safety Course

A Few words about us

Parho Education Foundation is a newly established organization with a mission to revolutionize education through innovative learning approaches, advanced technology, and a commitment to empowering the youth of Pakistan. We aim to address the pressing educational challenges in Pakistan, Our programs are designed to prepare students for the future by fostering skills in project-based learning, robotics, artificial intelligence (AI), and advanced technology.

Our Vision

To create a future where every child, regardless of their background, has access to innovative, technology-driven education, empowering them to become leaders, innovators, and changemakers in their communities and beyond.

Our Mission

To revolutionize education by fostering project-based learning, integrating advanced technologies like AI and robotics, and empowering students and teachers through training, scholarships, and community development programs, starting with underserved regions in Pakistan.

Our Goals

  • Enhance Educational Access: Establish modern educational programs to bridge the literacy gap in underserved communities, starting with Balochistan.
  • Foster Innovation: Introduce technology-focused curricula, such as robotics, AI, and data science, to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.
  • Empower Teachers: Provide comprehensive training programs to teachers, enabling them to adopt innovative teaching methodologies.
  • Promote Equal Opportunities: Offer scholarships and financial aid to deserving students, ensuring education is accessible to all.
  • Build Leadership: Develop leadership and critical thinking skills among students, preparing them to take on key roles in their communities.
  • Sustainability in Education: Create digital learning platforms and partnerships with other organizations to ensure long-term impact.
